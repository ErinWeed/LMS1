Come join us for the many adventures that are sure to come during the 2023 gardening season.

Weed Gardens is located in the Historic University District of Salt Lake City, Utah. We've been transforming the gardens for the past 13 years, and we're focused on producing bountiful harvests of produce and cut flowers-- all while using organic, sustainable, and water-wise practices.
